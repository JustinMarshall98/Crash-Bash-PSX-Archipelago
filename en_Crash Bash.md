
# Crash Bash

## What does randomization do to this game?

Randomizes rewards and progression for Adventure Mode.

## What is the goal of Crash Bash when randomized?

Defeat the Boss of your choosing!

## What items and locations get shuffled?

Trophies, Gems, Crystals, Gold Relics, Platinum Relics and Warp Room Access
are all items that are now shuffled into the multiworld.
Locations are all the minigame challenges across all the warp rooms in the game.

Minigames will show you what locations you have cleared, the pause menu will
show you an accurate count of each item you have.

## Which items can be in another player's world?

Trophies, Gems, Crystals, Gold Relics, Platinum Relics and Warp Room Access
are all items that are now shuffled into the multiworld.

## What does another world's item look like in Crash Bash

Upon completing a challenge you will acquire a multiworld item. In game there is no indication
as to what it was so you'll have to check the client.

## When the player receives an item, what happens?

Items are silently added to your inventory.
Hit Start to bring up the pause menu to check Trophies, Gems, Crystals, Gold and Platinum Relics.
Go to the Warp Room changer to see what Warp Rooms you have access to.
